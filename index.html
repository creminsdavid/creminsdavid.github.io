2018!

As I write this, my (18 y/o!) brother is playing piano, my dog is barking, my cat is staring wistfully into the fire, and my parents are doing parent-like stuff. We're heading into 2018 together and happily, excited and nervous for the changes to come. Both Kieran and I will graduate in May, go travel together, and then this summer I will begin working full-time for Workday in NorCal as a Software Engineer on the Student team :0

I still can't believe I got to this point, and will forever be grateful to the family, mentors, and friends from Houston and Claremont who gave me the skills and opportunities to somehow get to a school like Pomona and graduate from there with this incredible job. I am not sure that a long-term career in tech is in store for me necessarily, but I am going into this year and those to come open to a wide range of possible future paths. I may want to go back to school at some point for [something], after all :)

If you come across this page you are, according to Google Analytics, probably a Russian bot, interested in finding out about another David Cremins, or, and I hope this is true, someone I care about. If it is the latter, I wish you all the best for this year and hope we remain close as life takes us all in new and interesting directions!
