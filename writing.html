Excerpts

Linked above is a blog post containing some excerpts covering nearly a decade of my life, selected from various journals, notebooks, and school assignments. The thoughts throughout reveal parts of myself and my life that I had forgotten about, or not thought about for many years. The writing is, to me at least, often very funny, sometimes sad, but always honest. Undertaking this project, I admired how surprisingly aware of life I seemed to be from a young age, and embarrassed to read about some of my hypocrisies at the same time. For better or worse, I had no embarrassment about putting down on paper what was truly important to me back then, and I hope this will give you too a glimpse into who I am.
